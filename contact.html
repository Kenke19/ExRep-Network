Frequently Asked Questions

Get quick answers to common questions about partnering with EXREP Network

How quickly can EXREP Network help us launch in Nigeria?

We typically achieve full operational readiness in 6-8 weeks from contract signing, depending on your specific requirements and product complexity. Our streamlined processes and established local networks enable rapid deployment.

What industries do you work with?

We work with brands across e-commerce, technology, consumer goods, fashion, health & wellness, and creative services. Our operational expertise is adaptable to various sectors. If your industry isn't listed, contact us to discuss your specific needs - we're always exploring new opportunities.

Do you require any upfront capital investment?

No. Our partnership model requires zero capital investment from your company. We provide all necessary infrastructure and only charge operational fees based on usage. This risk-free approach allows you to test the market without significant financial commitment.

How do you ensure quality control with local manufacturers?

We have a rigorous vetting process for manufacturing partners and implement quality control checkpoints throughout the production process to ensure products meet international standards. Our quality assurance team conducts regular audits and provides detailed reporting to maintain transparency.
